About Me

Hi, I'm Jessica
I love transforming complex data into clear stories through impactful visualisations

My journey into data began with a fascination for numbers and patterns. I'm passionate about the power of visualisation to drive impactful change through data.

I thrive on tackling complex data challenges and transforming messy datasets into meaningful insights. Each dataset has its own story, and I specialise in bringing these narratives to life through effective visualisations.

Right now, I'm diving deeper into the captivating world of data science, sharpening my abilities as both a storyteller and a problem-solver through continuous study in the field.
